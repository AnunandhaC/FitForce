Workout Routines

Beginner

  • 3x/week Full body workout
  • Squats, Push-ups, Planks
  • 15-20 min cardio

Intermediate

  • 4x/week Split workout (Upper/Lower)
  • Deadlifts, Bench Press, Pull-ups
  • 20-30 min HIIT cardio

Advanced

  • 5-6x/week Targeted muscle groups
  • Weighted exercises & progressive overload
  • Cardio 30+ min or HIIT

Diet Plans

Weight Gain

  • High-protein breakfast (eggs, oats)
  • Snack: nuts & smoothie
  • Lunch: rice + chicken/fish + veggies
  • Dinner: quinoa + lentils + salad

Maintenance

  • Balanced carbs, protein, fats
  • Vegetables & fruits in each meal
  • Moderate portion sizes
  • Hydration: 2-3L water/day

Fat Loss

  • Low-calorie, high protein diet
  • Vegetables, lean meat, eggs
  • Limit sugar and processed foods
  • Hydrate & include fiber
